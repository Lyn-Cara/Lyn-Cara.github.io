Tobi Sunmola

Born in Lagos Nigeria, the young rapper discovered his fascination and inspiration for making music when he was 13 years old. Beginning his musical journey in Nigeria, he migrated to the UK at the age of 17 where he found a youth club that allowed him to create music for free and release music as a solo artiste under the alias ''Stripez''. His music evolved into melodies, production and songwriting.
